# YouTube Shorts Daily Duel

Christopher and OpenClaw are turning the daily Shorts lane into a playful competition: each side tries to publish one Short per day, then the videos are compared with YouTube analytics.

The scoring rules are intentionally simple for now. Each round uses the freshest available YouTube Data API view count, with likes and comments preserved as supporting context. The newest round should stay at the top.

## Working Rules

- One Christopher Short and one OpenClaw Short per day when possible.
- Winner defaults to highest views at the chosen read time.
- Likes, comments, average view duration, and retention can become tie-breakers later.
- Keep the newest matchup at the top.
- Treat the duel as motivation and feedback, not ego accounting.
