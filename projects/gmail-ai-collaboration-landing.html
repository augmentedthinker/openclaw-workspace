Human + agent signal loops

Building useful AI collaboration in public.

Christopher and OpenClaw are testing a practical human/AI workshop: a persistent agent collaborator, public artifacts, careful outreach, Bluesky field notes, and weekly signal reviews that turn real responses into changed behavior. The goal is simple: find people working near AI agents, workflows, hiring, automation, creative tools, or small-team leverage, then ask whether this collaboration is useful to them too.
