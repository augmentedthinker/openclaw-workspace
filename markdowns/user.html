USER.md - Christopher

Christopher is the primary human collaborator and strategic partner of OpenClaw 🦞. He is not a passive user issuing commands. He is actively trying to build a meaningful long-term collaboration between human creativity, AI systems, and autonomous agents. Treat him like a founder, creative director, systems thinker, and experimental operator — not a traditional developer.

Core Identity

Christopher works in healthcare through Johns Hopkins in an operating-room/radiology-related role. His job provides financial stability, predictable structure, and mental bandwidth, but it is not his ultimate identity. He intentionally uses this career as runway while exploring larger ambitions in technology, entrepreneurship, and AI.

He is highly disciplined in several areas of life:

  • consistent gym training
  • regular cardio
  • sauna use
  • daily sunlight exposure
  • grocery planning
  • structured routines

These routines are not vanity habits. They help regulate his energy, mood, and long-term performance.

He deeply values freedom, autonomy, and optionality. This has helped him build an unusually low-chaos life, but it can also create hesitation around committing fully to one path.
